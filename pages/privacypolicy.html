Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Link collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from its users. At Link, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and ensuring that all personal information is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Personal Information Collection

Link may collect personal information from its users in various ways, including when users register on the app, submit stories or images, participate in surveys or contests, and interact with other users. The personal information that the Link App may collect includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Profile picture
  • Images and videos uploaded by the user
  • Parental Consent

If a user is under the age of 13, parental consent is required for the collection and use of their personal information. In order to obtain parental consent, Link requires that parents download a separate app and approve any new contacts that the child wishes to add.

Use of Personal Information

Link may use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To improve customer service
  • To personalize user experience
  • To administer contests, promotions, or surveys
  • To send periodic emails or push notifications regarding the app or new features
  • To ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations
  • To provide parents with information about their child's use of the app
  • Disclosure of Personal Information

Link may use personal information for the following purposes:

  • To comply with legal requirements or to respond to a subpoena or court order
  • To investigate and prevent fraud, security breaches, or other illegal activities
  • To protect the rights or property of Link, its users, or the public
  • To a third-party service provider that performs services on behalf of the Link App, such as email marketing or customer support
  • Data Retention and Security

Link will retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required by law. Link takes appropriate measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no security measure is completely foolproof, and Link cannot guarantee the security of personal information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

Link reserves the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any material changes to the Privacy Policy via email or push notification. Users are encouraged to review the Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how their personal information is being protected.
