Help and Support

At Link, we want to ensure our users have the best possible experience with our app. We understand that sometimes things can go wrong, or you may have questions about using certain app features. That's why we offer various avenues for users to get help and support. Here are some of the ways you can get help and support for the Link App:

In-App Support

The first place to start is within the app itself. If you're having trouble with something or if you have a question about a particular feature, you can access our in-app support. To do this, go to the app settings and click "Help & Support." From there, you can browse our FAQ section or submit a support request. Our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.

Email Support

If you prefer to contact us via email, you can send an email to support@linkapp.com . Our support team will review your request and get back to you as soon as possible.

Community Forums

Lastly, we have a community forum where users can ask questions and get help from other users. This is a great way to get help from other users who may have experienced the same issues or have the same questions as you.

We are committed to providing our users with the best possible support at Link. We understand that using new apps can be daunting, so please don't hesitate to contact us if you need any help or have any questions.
