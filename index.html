Link - say hello to safe social media!

Introducing Link - the revolutionary social media app that brings peace of mind to parents and safety to children. Download Link today!

Link offers all the essential features you would expect from a social media app, including a profile page, story posting, and instant messaging with friends. Plus, with the added peace of mind that comes from the parent approval feature, Link sets itself apart as the must-have social media app for friends and families.

Tell your story

You can add pictures from their image gallery or take new photos using their phone's camera and upload them as stories which can be viewed by their added friends, while friends can also view the user's stories.

Chat with friends

Chat with your friends on Link, and invite the ones already on your phonebook to join! React to their messages and see them responding to you in real time!

Keep your kids safe

Parents with the Link app can link their child’s Link app with their own through a “parent” app. When the child wants to add a contact, the parents must approve the request, protecting them from adding or following strangers.
